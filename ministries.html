INTERNAL ORGANIZATION NOTE

Lantern Five is not formally listed as a ministry. Its orders nevertheless override every department whenever a document carries the valid five-light authentication pattern.

Some records describe L-5 as a person. Others call it an office, succession mechanism, council, or machine-assisted authority. The contradiction appears intentional.
