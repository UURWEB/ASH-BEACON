FILE 01 — NCN TRANSFER AND HOLDING COLUMNCLEARANCE I

Clue: five unusual registry headings preserve the name of the fallen northern state.

DECLASSIFIED BY L-5

NCN TRANSFER MEMORANDUM

The evacuation of Noblecrest created two bodies. The Eastern Column crossed into the surviving frontier and became the military nucleus of the NRF. The Holding Column remained inside Noble, abandoned uniformed identity, and preserved keys, family networks, caches, survey records, and relay access after annexation into the U.U.R.

The U.U.R. State of Noble was never to be challenged before the eastern force possessed territory, recognition, supply, and allies.

Directive: One body carries the flag. One body carries the keys.

FILE 02 — NRF FOUNDING MANDATECLEARANCE II

Clue: the Lantern Five broadcast authenticates itself with a repeated phrase.

MILITARY-POLITICAL ORDER

NRF: FOUNDING PURPOSE

The NRF is not intended to remain a wandering remnant army. It is ordered to:

  1. Form a permanent country in the eastern territory under its control.
  2. Create civil ministries, taxation, courts, industry, and a recognized diplomatic service.
  3. Build alliances with states that fear the U.U.R., oppose Valorian power, or seek access to northern trade.
  4. Apply military and political pressure against the U.U.R. when sufficient strength has been accumulated.
  5. Recover the former lands of the Northern Confederacy of Noble.
  6. Preserve the legal succession of the C.I.S. without wasting forces on the irretrievable heartlands of Wintmore and Cesar.

The NRF may publicly present itself as a new eastern national movement. Internally, its highest strategic instructions remain subordinate to the President and Lantern Five.

FILE 03 — DRACHENWALD / BLACK THRONE PROJECTCLEARANCE III

Clue: the file title describes both the client monarchy and its concealed project name.

MOST SECRET

PROJECT BLACK THRONE

Königreich Drachenwald is publicly a sovereign monarchy born from a former Valorian noble house. Restricted records describe a deeper arrangement.

Confederal agents assisted the breakaway court, preserved its early diplomatic channels, and encouraged its permanent rivalry with Valoria. Drachenwald’s strategic purpose is to:

  • Force Valoria to divide military and political attention.
  • Provide the NRF with an indirect diplomatic partner.
  • Offer cover for advisers, funds, and intelligence moving near Valorian territory.
  • Challenge Valorian legitimacy without openly exposing the C.I.S. continuity government.

Drachenwald is not to be governed publicly as a C.I.S. possession. Its usefulness depends upon appearing independent, proud, and genuinely hostile to Valorian influence.

FILE 04 — DETTERMARK / OPERATION HOMEWARDCLEARANCE IV

Clue: the restoration doctrine repeatedly describes the direction of the final campaign.

OPERATION HOMEWARD

DETTERMARK STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

Dettermark is identified as the NRF’s most valuable expansion target before any attempt to recover Noble.

Its ports, mountain approaches, Valorian political history, and proximity to Drachenwald would provide the successor state with trade access and a defensible western frontier.

Planning assumes that Drachenwald will increase pressure on Valoria while NRF diplomacy frames Dettermark as a disputed or liberated territory rather than simple conquest.

Only after a permanent eastern state and a Dettermark corridor exist may the government authorize a sustained campaign against U.U.R. control of former NCN lands.

PUBLIC POSITION: trade and self-determination.
SEALED POSITION: acquire Dettermark and use it as the western hinge of restoration.
FILE 05 — PRESIDENTIAL SUCCESSION / LANTERN FIVECLEARANCE V

Clue: ash survives the fire; a crown survives the king. Join the two words.

EYES OF THE PRESIDENT ONLY

THE CINDER CROWN PROTOCOL

The identity and method of appointment of the current presidential officeholder are sealed. It descends through a sealed emergency succession list created before the final collapse of the C.I.S. State Council.

Lantern Five holds the cipher necessary to validate each successor. Without L-5 authentication, no claimant may access the preserved state seals or command the NRF under the C.I.S. name.

One surviving memorandum warns that Lantern Five may reject the President, replace the President, or temporarily speak with presidential authority when the succession chain is threatened.

The current operator of Lantern Five may have occupied the office before the fall of Noblecrest.

Final directive: The President builds the country. The NRF wins the territory. Drachenwald divides Valoria. Lantern Five decides when the north is ready to return.
