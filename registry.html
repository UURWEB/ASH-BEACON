PORTABLE SOVEREIGNTY DOCTRINE

The continuity government distinguishes between historic territory and recoverable territory.

Wintmore and Cesar provide legitimacy, founding history, and state identity. The eastern NRF zone provides the land on which a functioning new country can actually be built. Noble and former NCN territory provide the central restoration objective.

The state therefore survives by moving its institutions before attempting to move its borders.
