OFFICIAL CONTINUITY GOVERNMENT HOME PAGE

Welcome to the Ash-Beacon gateway of the Confederacy of Independent States.

This service was established as a low-bandwidth civil and military gateway for use after the destruction or seizure of normal Confederal institutions. It continues to route executive notices, ministry forms, state claims, military liaison reports, and sealed continuity documents.

THE ORIGINAL CONFEDERAL HEARTLANDS OF WINTMORE AND CESAR ARE CLASSIFIED AS PERMANENTLY LOST TO ACTIVE RESTORATION PLANNING.

The present government therefore operates under the doctrine of portable sovereignty: the Confederacy survives through lawful succession, preserved state records, recognized offices, and the creation of a viable successor territory.

EXECUTIVE BULLETIN

PUBLIC RELEASE By order of the current President and under authentication from Lantern Five, all departments are to prioritize eastern civil organization, NRF diplomatic recognition, and the recovery of former Northern Confederacy of Noble territory.

Old claims over Wintmore and Cesar are to be maintained ceremonially but are not to consume personnel, arms, or diplomatic capital.

Policy formula: preserve the C.I.S. name, build a new country in the east, create alliances, weaken U.U.R. control in the north, and recover strategic lands once governed by the N.C.N.
